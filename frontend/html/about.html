Frequently Asked Questions

What image formats does Dalma AI accept?

We support JPG, PNG, and WEBP formats up to 10MB. For best results, use clear, well-lit photos with the dog as the main subject.

Can I use the generated models commercially?

Yes! All models generated with a paid subscription include a commercial license for use in games, animations, and other projects.

What 3D formats can I export?

Dalma AI supports GLB, FBX, OBJ, and USDZ formats, compatible with all major 3D software and game engines.

How accurate are the generated models?

Our AI captures the general shape, proportions, and characteristics of the dog in your photo. While not photogrammetry-level accurate, models are perfect for stylized games and applications.
